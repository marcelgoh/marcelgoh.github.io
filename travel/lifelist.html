Marcel Goh's fishing life list

This list includes only catches whose species is confirmed to a reasonable degree of accuracy (e.g., using iNaturalist). I have had observations that were only identifiable to the genus level, which I will not include here; likewise I do not count hybrids. In 2021 and 2022 I caught many common species (most of which were probably pumpkinseed, fallfish, and creek chub) that I didn't learn to identify until later. Everything in the list was caught on rod and line (including micro species such as stickleback!).
